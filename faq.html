What is The Metapass?
+

We want you to be a part of our community by becoming a Metaowner. As a closed community you must become a Metaown pass holder to be able to access the platform and its drops. Holding a Metapass grants you utility in each Metaown experience in various forms. A limited number of Metapsses have been and no additional passes will ever be created.
